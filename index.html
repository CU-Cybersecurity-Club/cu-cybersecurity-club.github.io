About Us: CU Boulder exists as one of the top research institutions in the US. Surrounding us is one of the largest concentrations of startups and tech companies nationally. With a consistent and long-running shortfall in the US cyber workforce, the industry is in desperate need of high-caliber cybersecurity professionals.

To this end, the CU Cyber Club seeks to provide students with the opportunity to compete in cybersecurity competitions, network with industry partners, develop offensive/defensive security skills, and become familiar with cyber tools and techniques. In addition to having fun, students will gain the skills, experiences, and connections they need to prepare for a career in cybersecurity.

Anyone of any skill level, major, or background is welcome! Be sure to join our discord community for the latest updates and events.
