About Us

CU Boulder exists as one of the top research institutions in the US. Surrounding this campus is one of the largest concentrations of startups and technology companies in the nation. With a national shortfall of 1.8 million cybersecurity professionals, CU Boulder is uniquely positioned to address this gap through the Technology, Cybersecurity and Policy program.

To meet this vision of delving high caliber cybersecurity professionals to our surrounding industry, we envision growing the value of our program through increased industry partnerships and research collaboration opportunities. We are currently looking to build these long term relationships through the assistance of establishing the first CU Boulder competitive cyber team and training environment.
